Privacy Policy for Nexus AI

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

Aetheltech ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Nexus AI mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle the permissions required for the app to function.

1. Data Collection & Permissions

Nexus AI is a custom interface and productivity tool. To provide its core features, the app requests the following device permissions:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Used exclusively for voice commands to interact with the AI assistant. Audio data is processed locally or sent securely to Google's Gemini API for transcription and response generation. We do not record or listen to audio in the background.
  • Camera: Used exclusively for scanning QR codes/barcodes to securely transfer configuration payloads (Neural Sync) between the PC and mobile unit. Images are processed locally and are never stored or uploaded to our servers.
  • Location (ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION / ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION): Used strictly while the app is actively on your screen to power the Heads-Up Display (HUD) Speedometer and Compass functionalities. Location data is processed locally on your device and is never stored, tracked, or shared.
  • Query All Packages: Used to populate the internal System Registry (App Drawer) so you can launch your installed applications directly from the Nexus interface.

2. Third-Party Services

The app connects to authorized third-party APIs based on user-provided keys. These include:

  • Google Gemini API: For processing AI chat and voice interactions.
  • Printify / Etsy APIs: For users utilizing the Merch Factory module.
  • Firebase: For cloud-syncing user preferences and validating Nexus Pro licenses.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements to non-Pro users.

3. Data Security & Storage

Your API keys, preferences, and chat history are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. If you create an account, basic authentication data is stored securely via Firebase. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal data to data brokers or marketing agencies.
